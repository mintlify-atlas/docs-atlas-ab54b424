<Warning>
  **This is not a medical diagnosis tool.** HealthIA suggests educational hypotheses only. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for any medical concern.
</Warning>

## Official disclaimer

Every response from the HealthIA system includes the following disclaimer, returned in the `disclaimer` field of every API response:

```text
Projeto educacional. Esta ferramenta sugere hipóteses de estudo e não substitui avaliação médica profissional.
```

*English translation: Educational project. This tool suggests study hypotheses and does not replace professional medical evaluation.*

## What HealthIA is

HealthIA is an educational triage assistant. It accepts free-text symptom descriptions and optional patient context, then returns a ranked list of probable conditions based on a trained ML classifier. Its purpose is to help users form a starting point for a conversation with a healthcare professional — not to conclude that conversation.

The system is designed for:

- **Educational exploration** of symptom patterns and related conditions
- **Triage orientation**, giving users a sense of which medical domains are relevant to their symptoms
- **Context-aware suggestions**, using patient demographics and history to improve relevance

## What HealthIA is not

HealthIA does not:

- Diagnose diseases or medical conditions
- Recommend treatments, medications, or dosages
- Issue or suggest prescriptions
- Replace clinical examination or laboratory testing
- Provide emergency medical guidance

The system returns **differential suggestions** (hypotheses), not conclusions. A high confidence score means the symptom pattern is statistically consistent with a condition — it does not mean the condition is present.

## Low-confidence behavior

When the input is too sparse, ambiguous, or non-clinical for the system to generate a reliable suggestion, the API returns an empty `top_predictions` list and the following warning:

```text
Informações insuficientes para sugestão confiável
```

*English translation: Insufficient information for a reliable suggestion.*

This occurs when:

- The symptom text is too short or contains no recognizable clinical terms
- Too few TF-IDF features are non-zero after preprocessing
- The top prediction confidence falls below the minimum threshold

In these cases, no condition names are displayed and users are encouraged to provide more detail.

## Emergency warnings

When the highest-confidence prediction is flagged as an emergency condition (`is_emergency: true`) and its confidence is at or above 0.6, the API returns the following warning alongside the predictions:

```text
Foram encontradas hipóteses potencialmente urgentes. Procure avaliação profissional imediata se houver piora clínica.
```

*English translation: Potentially urgent hypotheses were found. Seek immediate professional evaluation if symptoms worsen.*

This warning does not mean an emergency is confirmed. It means the symptom pattern is statistically consistent with a condition that can be serious, and that professional evaluation should not be delayed.

## The MedicalDisclaimer UI component

The frontend renders a `MedicalDisclaimer` component on every page where results are displayed. It is always visible and cannot be dismissed:

```tsx
export function MedicalDisclaimer() {
  return (
    <div
      role="note"
      aria-label="Aviso médico"
    >
      <strong>Aviso médico:</strong> Esta ferramenta é de apoio e não substitui avaliação médica profissional.
    </div>
  );
}
```

*English translation of the displayed text: Medical notice: This tool is for support and does not replace professional medical evaluation.*

This component is rendered independently of the prediction result — it appears whether or not the model returns suggestions.

## When to seek immediate medical attention

Do not rely on HealthIA if you are experiencing:

- Chest pain, pressure, or tightness
- Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
- Sudden severe headache
- Loss of consciousness or altered mental state
- Signs of stroke (face drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulty)
- Severe abdominal pain
- High fever with stiff neck or rash
- Any symptom that is rapidly worsening

Call emergency services or go to the nearest emergency room immediately in these situations.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I use HealthIA to decide whether to see a doctor?">
    No. HealthIA can help you understand which medical domains may be relevant to your symptoms, but it cannot determine whether your situation requires medical attention. When in doubt, consult a healthcare professional.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why does the system show a high confidence score but still include a disclaimer?">
    Confidence scores reflect statistical consistency between your symptom pattern and a condition in the training dataset — not a clinical certainty. Many conditions share overlapping symptoms, and the model has no access to examination findings, lab results, or imaging. The disclaimer is always present regardless of confidence.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What does 'educational hypothesis' mean?">
    A hypothesis is a starting point for investigation, not a conclusion. HealthIA frames its output as study hypotheses — conditions worth discussing with a doctor — rather than diagnoses. The phrasing is deliberate: it signals that the output is for informational orientation, not clinical decision-making.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does HealthIA recommend treatments or medications?">
    No. The system returns condition names, confidence scores, severity indicators, and an explanation of why the top condition was suggested. It never recommends treatments, medications, dosages, or any clinical intervention.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
