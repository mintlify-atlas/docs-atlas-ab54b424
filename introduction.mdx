## What is HealthIA?

HealthIA is a full-stack AI application that analyzes free-text symptom descriptions and optional patient context to suggest likely conditions in an educational triage setting. It combines natural language processing, machine learning, and a modern SaaS-style interface to deliver context-aware insights safely and transparently.

The system targets Portuguese clinical language, making it accessible to Brazilian users who want to better understand their symptoms before (or alongside) seeking professional care.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe your symptoms">
    Enter a free-text description of your symptoms in natural language. The system handles noisy, incomplete, or colloquial input through NLP preprocessing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Optionally add patient context">
    Provide optional demographic and clinical context — age, sex, pregnancy status, comorbidities, medications, and more — to improve prediction relevance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive differential suggestions">
    The ML pipeline returns the top probable conditions with confidence scores, severity indicators, and emergency flags.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the explanation">
    HealthIA explains why the top condition was suggested, citing matched symptoms and relevant patient context.
  </Step>
</Steps>
